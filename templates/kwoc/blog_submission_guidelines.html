Congratulations! on successfully clearing your end evaluations.

As we approach the conclusion of Kharagpur Winter of Code 2024, we would like to remind you about the report submission and the corresponding guidelines to be followed.

This report provides an overview of your contributions throughout the 5-week period and is an important component of our evaluation.

The report can be as descriptive as you want but must include the following points:

  • List of projects you worked on.
  • List of pull requests you have created, along with links to the PRs.
  • A concise yet informative summary of your work and your learnings.

You can publish this in a blog-like format on any platform you choose, like Medium or Github Gist and be sure to submit the link to your report through the report submission form in the student dashboard.

Feel free to look for inspiration from the previous testimonials present on the landing page.

Report Submission Link would be open soon on the student dashboard.

The deadline for submitting your report is January 15th.

Failing to submit the report by this date will result in a failed participation.
